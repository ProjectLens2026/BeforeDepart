📱 Travel Apps You Actually Need

By BeforeDepart Editorial Team · Updated June 2026 · 5 min read

A lean toolkit of apps that genuinely help — set them up before you leave home.

Quick facts: Tech · 5 min read · Updated June 2026. Practical, original travel advice you can use on any trip.

A lean travel toolkit

You don't need dozens of apps — just a few that genuinely help. Set these up before you leave.

Getting around

  • Offline maps (download your destination before you fly).
  • A ride-hailing app that works locally.
  • Your airline's app for boarding passes and alerts.

Staying connected

  • An eSIM app so you have data on arrival.
  • A translation app with offline language packs.
  • A messaging app to keep in touch with home.

Money & planning

  • Your banking app (and a card with no foreign fees).
  • A currency converter.
  • BeforeDepart to research safety, essentials, food and guides for any destination.

Set up before you go

Download everything and any offline packs on home Wi-Fi, log in, and test them. Landing somewhere new with your maps, money and data ready makes everything easier.
